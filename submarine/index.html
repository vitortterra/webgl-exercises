Keyboard controls:

Translation:

J: move forward

L: move backwards

K: stop translation

Rotation:

W: pitch up

X: pitch down

D: yaw to the right

A: yaw to the left

C: roll clockwise

Z: roll counter-clockwise

S: stop rotation

Change view:

M: change POV to next submarine

N: change POV to previous submarine
